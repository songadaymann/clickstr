How to Play
The Basics

Click the button to mine $CLICK. Each click does real cryptographic work (proof-of-work) to earn tokens.

How It Works
  • Connect your wallet
  • Click the button
  • Once you've clicked 50 and up to 500 times, submit a transaction
  • You pay gas to claim your $CLICK tokens
The 50/50 Burn

Every token earned also burns a token. If you earn 100 $CLICK, another 100 is burned forever. More clicking = scarcer token.

Seasons & Epochs

The game runs in seasons divided into epochs (24 hours). Each epoch, 2% of the remaining pool is distributed. Top clicker each epoch gets a 10% bonus.

NFT Rewards

Hit click milestones to unlock collectible NFTs and custom cursors. Check your Mint Rewards panel and Collection to see what you've earned.

Scripts Welcome

You can also click directly via the smart contract using our bot script (or your own custom one). But only frontend clicks count toward NFT rewards and milestones. Scripts earn $CLICK, humans earn $CLICK + collectibles.

Between Seasons

Even without an active season, your frontend clicks are still tracked and count toward NFT milestones. You just won't earn $CLICK until the next season starts.
